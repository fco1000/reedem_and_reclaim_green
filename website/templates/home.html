Who we are

We are Environmental Stewards founded in the belief that we are charged with the responsibility to take care of the environment. (Genesis 2:15 : The Lord God took the man and put him in the Garden of Eden to work it and take care of it).

Our Vision

To redeem and reclaim the barren lands in semi-arid, arid and deserts into useful and arable lands that can sustain life.

Our Mission

To promote healthy living, access to water, offer relief and manage disasters and contribute to environmental conservation.

Objectives

In a world where barren lands spread and invasive species choke life, Redeem and Reclaim Green stands as a beacon of hope. Discover our urgent mission to reclaim the Earth and ensure survival. Learn More!

Core Values

  • We are Environmental Stewards: We believe that the world ecosystem needs to be healthy for existence.
  • We are all-time Good Samaritans: We have compassion and empathy towards humans and other living beings by ensuring they live healthy in their habitats.
  • We believe in teamwork: We believe that there is power in unity. When together, we generate viable ideas. We have open windows for consultations and assistance meant to make the world a better place to live in.
