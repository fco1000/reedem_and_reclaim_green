Objectives

  1. To control the spread of invasive harmful plant species and promote afforestation: establishing afforestation with an aim to reduce soil erosion and attract rainfall by managing and controlling the spread of invasive and harmful plant species especially Prosopis Julifora which has invaded most fertile portions of land.

  2. To promote disaster awareness and preparedness: enlightening communities in arid, semi-amid and desert areas on negative effects of disasters such as floods droughts and famine through establishment of effective long lasting capacity building approaches and strategies such as trainings in preparation for their occurrence.

  3. To promote food security and good health: educating communities in arid, semi arid and desert areas on the relationship between food insecurity and health outcomes such as malnutrition.

  4. To establish enough water catchment points: introducing water management and conservation techniques such as floods water collection system and run-off water harvesting and utilization of solar energy in arid, semi-arid and desert areas to generate water from boreholes and other available water points and make it readily available for domestic use.

  5. To promote sustainable agriculture to eradicate poverty: promoting and supporting sustainable agriculture developments in the fight against poverty to ensure the affected communities in arid, semi-amid and desert areas enjoy food security.
